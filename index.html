Why These Companies?

Target Profile: ADA-IR Emissions Monitoring Solutions

ADA-IR provides infrared-based continuous emissions monitoring systems (CEMS) and gas analyzers for industrial compliance with Malaysia's Environmental Quality Act 1974 and DOE regulations.

Heavy Industry & Manufacturing: Cement (CIMA, YTL, Lafarge), Steel (Southern Steel, Ann Joo), Glass (Malaya Glass, Xinyi) - High-temperature processes emitting NOx, SOx, CO, particulates requiring continuous monitoring.
Petrochemicals & Chemicals: Petronas Chemicals, Lotte Chemical, Scientex - Complex gas mixtures, VOCs, flare stack monitoring, process safety compliance.
Palm Oil & Agro-processing: IOI Group, Sime Darby Plantation, QL Resources - Biomass boilers, POME treatment, methane capture, RSPO sustainability compliance.
Food & Beverage: Nestlé, F&N, PPB Group - Steam boilers, refrigeration systems, energy efficiency reporting, ESG commitments.
Automotive & Electronics: UMW/Sime, Inari Amertron - Paint booths (VOCs), soldering fumes, cleanroom compliance, ISO 14001.
Pharmaceuticals: Duopharma - Solvent recovery, cleanroom emissions, GMP environmental monitoring.
Power & Utilities: Genting, Malakoff, Tenaga affiliates - Combustion monitoring, CEMS mandatory for >50MW plants.
Key Insight: Malaysia's DOE increasingly mandates CEMS for Category 1-3 premises. Companies with public ESG commitments (FTSE4Good, Bursa Sustainability) have budget allocation and internal champions for emissions tech upgrades.
